## SUSECon Insights

Market Insights from SUSE and IDC

- 60–70% of all applications are NOT modernized yet. This means there is still a lot of work to do—and this percentage refers to the number of applications, not infrastructure.
- More than 70% of modernization work still needs to be done, and these are likely the most complex applications.
- Even modern applications are becoming legacy due to the slow pace of development.
